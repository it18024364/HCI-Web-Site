Room Specifications



The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels,a kettle, a bidet,
a hairdryer and a desk. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries,
rooms at the hotel also boast free WiFi. The units have a wardrobe.
The hotel has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre and bar.


Most popular facilities

The Coffee Shop serves a daily buffet breakfast, while the rooftop California Grill
offers fresh seafood and steaks. Other dining options include Chinese cuisine at
Stix, Arabian food at Sheherezade Restaurant and pastries at Cafe 64. Drinks are
available at the hotel's 2 bars and karaoke lounge.


The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.
